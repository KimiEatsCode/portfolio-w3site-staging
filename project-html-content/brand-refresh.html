Brand Refresh

For PhilaWorks Brand Refresh, I collaborated with a contract designer to create ideas for new brand elements for updating communications materials. I utilized collaborative tools of Canva and Adobe to work with the contractor so we could contribute asynchronously since we had two different schedules. I helpd virtual design meetings to brainstorm ideas, share design inspiration, and confirm our final designs. I designed a power point presentation to present the final brand colors and design elements to stakeholders for their sign off.
